HakkımızdaAbout Us

Küçük stüdyo, büyük fikirler.

na²games, oyun yapmanın özündeki şeye — iyi tasarım, temiz mekanikler ve gerçek eğlenceye — inanan bağımsız bir stüdyodur.

Karmaşıklık değil, netlik arıyoruz. Her oyunumuz, oyuncunun ekrana baktığı ilk saniyeden itibaren sezgisel ve tatmin edici hissetmeli.

Türkiye'den dünyaya ulaşan oyunlar yapıyoruz — çok dilli destek, evrensel tasarım anlayışı.

Small studio, big ideas.

na²games is an independent studio that believes in the essence of game making — good design, clean mechanics, and genuine fun.

We seek clarity, not complexity. Every game we make should feel intuitive and satisfying from the very first second a player looks at the screen.

We make games that reach the world from Turkey — multilingual support, universal design thinking.

Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

Our games store your game progress (completed levels, scores, gold amount, purchased items) locally on your device only. This data is not sent to our servers.

2. Advertisements

Our applications use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Children's Privacy

Our applications do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

4. Data Security

Game data is stored locally and encrypted on your device to prevent unauthorized access. Internet connection is only used for ad delivery and time validation for daily rewards.

Política de Privacidad

1. Datos Recopilados

Nuestros juegos almacenan tu progreso de juego (niveles completados, puntuaciones, cantidad de oro, objetos comprados) únicamente en tu dispositivo. Estos datos no se envían a nuestros servidores.

2. Publicidad

Nuestra aplicación utiliza Google AdMob para mostrar anuncios. AdMob puede recopilar identificadores de dispositivo y datos de uso para mostrar anuncios personalizados. Para más información, consulta la Política de Privacidad de Google.

3. Privacidad de los Niños

Nuestra aplicación no recopila intencionalmente información personal de menores de 13 años.

4. Seguridad de Datos

Los datos del juego se almacenan localmente y están encriptados en su dispositivo para evitar accesos no autorizados. La conexión a internet solo se utiliza para la entrega de anuncios y la validación del tiempo para recompensas diarias.
